# VideoDirector: Precise Video Editing via Text-to-Video Models

- **Year**: 2025
- **Authors**: Lee et al.
- **Conference/Source**: CVPR 2025
- **Topic**: Generative Video Editing
- **Status**: Top Tier

## Core Contribution
Enables direct video editing using Text-to-Video models with spatial-temporal decoupled guidance. Allows precise inversion and editing of specific objects.

## Relevance to Project
Shows how we can move from "cutting" to "generating" new frames based on transcript changes.
